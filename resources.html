Resources

A curated list of free software, assets and references to help you build isometric rooms.

Software

  • Blender

    Free, open-source 3D creation suite for modeling, lighting and rendering.

  • MagicaVoxel

    Free, lightweight voxel editor with a built-in path-traced renderer.

  • GIMP

    Free image editor, useful for touching up renders and creating textures.

  • Krita

    Free digital painting tool, great for creating custom textures and palettes.

Assets & references

  • Free texture libraries

    Look for sites offering CC0 (public domain) textures for materials like wood, fabric and metal.

  • HDRI environments

    Free HDRI maps can give your Blender renders realistic ambient lighting in just a few clicks.

  • Color palettes

    Curated color palette tools help keep your isometric scenes visually cohesive.

Learning more

  • Official Blender Manual

    The official documentation covers every tool and feature in depth.

  • MagicaVoxel community tutorials

    Search video platforms for "MagicaVoxel isometric room" for a wide range of styles and techniques.
